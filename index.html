About Me

 

Hi, I’m Paige! I’m a web developer and graphic designer. My knowledge and experience allows me to bridge the gap between code and design. Each project is a new opportunity in the making. My goal is to produce an ideal design experience through a cohesive relationship between the creative, the technical, and the client and end user. To do this I do my best to provide an ideal design through collaboration and creativity.

I graduated in Spring 2020 with two degrees in Web Development and Graphic Design from Southwestern Illinois College. Before that I went to Eastern Illinois University for my Bachelor of Music. When I'm not working you can find me rock climbing, reading, playing Dungeons and Dragons with friends, and creating art.
