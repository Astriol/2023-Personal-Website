About Me


Hi, I’m Paige! I’m a web developer and graphic designer. Most days you can find me rock climbing, reading, playing Dungeons and Dragons with friends, and creating art. I graduated in Spring 2020 with two degrees in Web Development and Graphic Design from Southwestern Illinois College. Before that I went To Eastern Illinois University for my Bachelor of Music.

My specialty knowledge allows me to bridge the gap between code and design. Each project I take on is a new creative challenge and my goal is to create a cohesive relationship between code, design, and user. To do this I do my best to provide an ideal design through collaboration and creativity.
